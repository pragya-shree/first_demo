The Problem

Every year, billions of tons of food go to waste globally. Much of this waste happens at home simply because we forget about what's in our fridge or pantry until it's too late. This not only wastes money but also has a significant environmental impact.

Our Solution

Perishable Food Optimizer is a smart inventory management tool designed to minimize food waste. By tracking expiry dates and providing a clear, color-coded dashboard, it helps you prioritize consuming items that are closest to expiration, saving you money and helping the planet.

About Our Initiative

Our organization was founded with the vision of reducing food waste while ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it the most. Millions of meals are wasted daily due to poor coordination and time-sensitive logistics. We aim to solve this gap using technology.

By connecting donors, NGOs, and delivery networks through an optimized routing system, we ensure food is picked up and delivered before expiry. Our platform prioritizes time-critical donations to maximize impact.
